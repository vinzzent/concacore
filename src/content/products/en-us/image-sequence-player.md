Our custom visual for **Power BI**. The Image Sequence Player allows you to animate and seamlessly play a sequence of images directly in your data dashboards, transforming static reports into dynamic visual storytelling.

## Features
* **Filter on Play (Cross-Filtering & Highlighting):** Dynamically filter and highlight other visuals in your report as the image sequence progresses.
* **Advanced Playback Controls:** Autoplay, loop, custom frame durations, and step-by-step navigation.
* **High-Performance Rendering:** Built-in image preloading and intelligent caching for a seamless, buffer-free experience.
* **Rich Customization:** Smooth transitions (Fade/Slide), customizable progress dots, and dynamic data-driven captions.
