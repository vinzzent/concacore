## Unified Data Architecture
A reliable analytics strategy starts with a solid foundation. We design and build end-to-end data pipelines on Fabric's OneLake — handling ETL/ELT engineering, custom Python extraction scripts, API integrations, and complex relational databases — delivering a single, governed source of truth your entire organization can trust.

## Insight-to-Action Applications
Spotting a trend is only half the job. We embed interactive business applications directly into your Power BI environment, giving teams the ability to update records, adjust forecasts, or trigger approvals without ever leaving their data view. No tab-switching. No friction.

## Event-Driven Automation
We make your data proactive. By monitoring your Fabric data models in real time, we design intelligent workflows that automatically fire the moment a threshold is crossed — alerting the right people, triggering reorders, or dispatching tailored reports before a human even notices the shift.

## Custom Visuals & Embedded Portals
When standard charts aren't enough, we build them from scratch. We develop custom Power BI visuals engineered to match exactly how your business thinks and measures its KPIs. We elevate this customization through **ultralight Power BI Embedded** solutions. Instead of forcing your company to adopt monolithic, comprehensive third-party portals—where you would pay a premium for countless features you'll never use—we create surgical, tailor-made data portals designed for your exact needs. The major advantage is sovereignty: the solution stays with you, running in your own environment, eliminating the dependency and recurring costs of external off-the-shelf services. Whether for a focused public portal or a secure environment for partners, we deliver the exact insight without the extra bloat.

## AI-Integrated Data Ecosystem
AI is not a feature we bolt on — it's a layer we design in from the start. Across the entire data lifecycle, we identify where intelligence creates real leverage: automating and monitoring data pipeline operations, detecting anomalies and patterns at a scale no human analyst can match, generating narratives and summaries directly from your reports, and enabling your teams to explore data through natural language instead of SQL queries. The result is an ecosystem that doesn't just surface what happened — it actively helps your organization understand why, predict what's next, and act faster.

## Lean Web Applications & Custom APIs
Analytics is only one piece of the puzzle. To bring your data to life, we build secure web applications and APIs with an absolute focus on architectural efficiency. We believe in solutions tailored to your exact needs: as lightweight, fast, and efficient as possible. Instead of overloading the project with heavy frameworks or unnecessary databases (especially when the focus is solely on consuming already processed data), we adopt a performance-driven philosophy. We use high-speed asynchronous backends, such as **Fastify** or FastAPI, paired with modern, ultralight frontend approaches like **Astro** and **HTMX**. The result is sustainable applications and microservices, free from over-engineering, that seamlessly integrate your business logic and embedded reports with maximum speed and minimum infrastructure cost.
