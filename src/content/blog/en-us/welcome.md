# Welcome to the Concacore Blog

This is our new unified space where you can find all the content you need. Markdown articles and structured resources are now combined into a single, seamless experience.

## Why We Built This

We wanted a system that provides:
1. **Unification:** No more hunting across different pages for videos, PDFs, and articles.
2. **Global Reach:** Deep internationalization built from the ground up to support our `en-US` and `pt-BR` audiences.
3. **Future Readiness:** Our structured data architecture is strictly designed to plug right into an AI-driven translation pipeline because identity is locked to the unique IDs instead of file names or titles.
